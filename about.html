About LBTC


The Little Bird Toy Company is a small, family-owned business specializing in handcrafted wooden toys for children. Based in a charming small town, the siblings behind the company, Evan, Abigail, and Elizabeth, have been creating unique and heirloom-quality toys for the past three years. Their passion for quality craftsmanship and fostering children's imaginations has garnered them a loyal local following. With growing popularity and encouragement from friends and family, The Little Bird Toy Company is looking to expand their reach beyond their local community. They recognize the potential of the internet to connect them with customers nationwide and share their love for handcrafted toys.
